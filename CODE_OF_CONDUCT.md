# Code of Conduct

We are committed to building a kind, respectful, and collaborative community.
This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull
requests, discussions, and any community channels.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Assume good intentions and offer constructive feedback
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users
- Help others learn and contribute

We do not tolerate:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior
- Personal attacks or insulting language
- Publishing private information without consent

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the
maintainers at rafaeljkey@gmail.com. We will review reports promptly and
fairly, and we may take action to keep the community safe and welcoming.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for interpreting and enforcing this code
of conduct. They may remove comments, edit content, or restrict participation
as needed to uphold these standards.

Thank you for helping make this project a welcoming place for everyone.
