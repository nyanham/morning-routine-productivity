# Code of Conduct

> Our shared standards for a kind, respectful, and collaborative community.

---

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to **all** project spaces ‚ÄÅEissues, pull
requests, discussions, and any associated community channels.

---

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and considerate in every interaction.
- Assume good intentions and offer constructive feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Help others learn and contribute.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile conduct.
- Personal attacks or insulting language.
- Publishing another person's private information without consent.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please contact the
maintainers at **rafaeljkey@gmail.com**. Reports are reviewed promptly and
fairly, and appropriate action will be taken to keep the community safe and
welcoming.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for interpreting and enforcing this code
of conduct. They may remove comments, edit content, or restrict participation
as needed to uphold these standards.

---

Thank you for helping make this project a welcoming place for everyone.
